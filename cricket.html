Sri Lanka Cricket Team

About the Team

The Sri Lanka national cricket team, nicknamed The Lions, represents Sri Lanka in men's international cricket. It is a Full Member of the International Cricket Council (ICC) with Test, One-Day International (ODI) and T20 International (T20I) status.

Key Players

Dasun Shanaka

All-rounder and current captain of the T20I and ODI teams

Wanindu Hasaranga

All-rounder known for his leg spin bowling

Dhananjaya de Silva

Top-order batsman and off-spin bowler

Achievements

  • ICC Cricket World Cup champions (1996)
  • ICC T20 World Cup champions (2014)
  • ICC Champions Trophy champions (2002)
